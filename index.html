About Me

Hey! I'm Gerald, and I'm a Phoenix based indie game designer. I've made two games so far: a timed idle game (I swear it makes sense just trust me bro), and a typing roguelike.

I love roguelikes, so that's naturally what a lot of my games end up as (even if it's unintentional). I love doing Pokemon challenge runs, and I think that's part of where my passion for roguelikes comes from, as odd as it seems.

At the moment, I'm pretty in-between projects. Not sure what I wanna work on yet.
